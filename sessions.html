Process

1. Plan

After booking, you receive a prep guide covering outfits, references, and how to prepare for camera. We then finalise location options, timing based on best light, and a clear visual direction so you arrive fully prepared.

2. Shoot

On session day, I guide you through posing, movement, and expression in a relaxed way so nothing feels forced. We work through planned shots first, then create spontaneous frames to capture more personality and variety.

3. Edit

Every selected image is colour-balanced and refined with hand retouching where needed. The final set is edited for consistency across skin tones, lighting, and mood, while keeping a natural and authentic look.

4. Deliver

Your final gallery is delivered online with high-resolution and web-ready files for easy sharing. Standard delivery is 7-10 days for portrait and lifestyle sessions, and 5-7 days for automotive shoots.
